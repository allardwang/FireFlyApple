FireFly Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

  • Registration & Login: We collect your email address for identity verification.
  • Usage Data: We collect app crash logs and feature usage statistics to improve your experience.
  • Payment Information: In-app purchases are processed by Apple; we do not collect payment information.

2. Use of Information

  • Your email is used to send verification codes and important notifications.
  • Crash logs are used to fix bugs.
  • Usage statistics are used to optimize features.

3. Information Storage

  • Financial data such as holdings, dividends, and FIRE configurations is stored locally on your device by default.
  • Logged-in users can choose to sync data to our cloud server.
  • Servers use encrypted storage, and you can delete your account at any time to clear cloud data.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

This app has no advertisements, no analytics, and no third-party data collection SDKs.

5. Your Rights

  • View and modify your profile.
  • Export your holdings data.
  • Delete your account, and all cloud data will be permanently erased.

6. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time, and you will be notified within the app after updates.

7. Contact Us

Email: monsterallard@gmail.com

本应用不会将您的个人信息出售、交易或转让给第三方。

本App无广告、无统计、无第三方数据采集SDK。

五、您的权利

  • 查看和修改个人资料。
  • 导出持仓数据。
  • 删除账户，所有云端数据将被永久清除。

本协议可能不时更新，更新后将在应用内通知。
